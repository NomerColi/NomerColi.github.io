Cicada

Cicada is a platformer set in post-apocalyptic world. I developed monsters, flashlight, and some other things. It was a journey to explore Unity 2D physics lighting system.

The games makes players to go through dangerous over-grown creatures and reach to flags.

There was much more monsters I developed than in the final version, mantis, parrot, and even wild boar boss monster. But unfortunately they didn't fit in our level designs.
